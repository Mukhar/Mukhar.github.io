About

Engineer first, problem-solver always.

I graduated from IIT Delhi in 2021 and have spent the last five years building systems that quietly carry a lot of weight — onboarding platforms used by thousands, secrets managers that touch every microservice, and data pipelines that feed the dashboards leadership reads every morning.

At Walmart Labs I work across the stack — Java and Spring Boot for the heavy lifting, Go for systems work, React for the UI, and Kubernetes & GCP underneath. Lately I’ve been focused on bringing GenAI into developer workflows, where the gains compound.

Before Walmart, I spent three years at Standard Chartered GBS as a full-stack SDE 2 — leading a Java 8 → 17 modernization, rebuilding the bank’s digital onboarding for global customers, and saving the business $1M a year by redesigning a single PDF utility.

Outside of work I’ve spoken at DevOps India Summit, finished in the top 1% of the AWS DeepRacer Global Championship, and spent a summer at SciLifeLab, Sweden, building software for DNA sequencing.
